Design Usability, User-Interface/User-Experience (UI/UX), Mobile Applications, Content Management Systems, and web hosting are level-up concepts after Web Development. These are all concepts that would be associated with Front-End Development.

Site development is important if you want folks to not only visit your site but utilize what you are offering. If you are looking to sell items you want folks to come, see, stay, buy and come back to your site. In order for that to happen users need to feel comfortable with your site. They want you site to load quickly and have the information presented in a format that is most appealing to them. The site should also be able to serve mobile users. Developer tools now incorporate different mobile templates for building and tested for mobile users.

Content Management is important because you want to keep your site up to date and if you happen to be working with someone else on your site, you want to keep things in order between the two of you as users.
